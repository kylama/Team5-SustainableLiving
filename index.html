Impact!

Environmental Damage

Food waste contributes to greenhouse gas emissions, deforestation, and water waste.

Economic Loss

Households, businesses, and governments lose billions annually due to discarded food.

Hunger and Inequality

While food is wasted, millions of people face food insecurity and malnutrition.
